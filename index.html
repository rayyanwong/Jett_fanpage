JETT

Welcome to my Jett fanpage!

Jett is a VALORANT agent who specializes in mobility and assassinations.

Jett's abilities

ABILITIES

Cloudburst

Instantly throw your projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. Hold the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair.

Updraft

Instantly propel Jett high into the air.

Tailwind

INSTANTLY propel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she will propel forward.

Blade storm

Equip a set of highly accurate throwing knives that recharge on killing an opponent. Fire to throw a single knife at your target. Alternate fire to throw all remaining daggers at your target.
